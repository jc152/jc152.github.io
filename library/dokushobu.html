読書部

いっしょに読もう！

how does it work?

Every month we pick a beginner and intermediate book to read through together at a chapter per weekend. A book stays in it's spot monthly until it's finished. Then new voting begins to replace it.

Come and discuss reading Japanese with us on Discord.

how to make the most out of this

Read using your computer:

  • using the novel library
  • combined with a pop-up dictionary like Yomichan, Rikaichan [firefox] [chrome], or Rikaisama [older firefox browsers]

Reading using your Android device:

  • downloading the epub of the novel from the library
  • combined with a reader app such as Typhon, an ebook reader with built in pop-up dictionary support.
